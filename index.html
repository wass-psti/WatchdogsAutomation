Industrial automation built for field conditions.

Watchdog Automation delivers PLC programming, HMI and SCADA integration, instrumentation, panel modernization, commissioning support, and industrial control solutions for utilities, manufacturing, and facility operations.

PLC & SCADA Engineering

Design, programming, migration, and integration of PLC and SCADA systems for real-time monitoring, diagnostics, and control.

Instrumentation & Commissioning

Installation support, loop checking, calibration, signal verification, and structured commissioning for field-ready deployment.

Retrofit & Modernization

Upgrade of legacy control systems, control panels, drives, and interfaces to current industrial platforms with maintainable handover.

How We Work

Predictable delivery beats debugging in production. We favor clear scope, testable outputs, and clean handover.

01 • Discovery

Review I/O, drawings, network architecture, constraints, and acceptance criteria.

02 • Design

Define cause and effect, alarm philosophy, screen list, and validation plan.

03 • Build

Implement with version control, technical review, and structured test checkpoints.
