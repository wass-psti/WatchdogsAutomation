BMS Integration

Chiller and Pump Integration
to Building Management System

Project Overview

Integration of chillers, pumps, and cooling tower controls into an existing Building Management System using PLC interface and industrial communication protocols.

Background & Challenge

The facility's chillers, pumps, and cooling towers were operating as standalone systems with no connection to the central Building Management System. This made it difficult for facility managers to monitor equipment status, detect faults early, or optimize energy consumption from a single platform. The project required careful coordination between the BMS platform, the new PLC interface layer, and the existing mechanical equipment to achieve reliable centralized control.
