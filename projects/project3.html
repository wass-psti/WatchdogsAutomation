PLC Migration

Ginebra San Miguel
Mandaue Plant

Project Overview

Upgrading the Mandaue plant's automation system by migrating from the legacy Siemens S7-300 PLC to the modern S7-1500 series, enhancing performance, diagnostics, and long-term maintainability.

Background & Challenge

The Ginebra San Miguel Mandaue Plant relied on an aging Siemens S7-300 PLC system to control critical production processes. As the platform approached end-of-life, maintaining the system became increasingly challenging due to limited spare parts availability and lack of support for modern communication protocols. A migration to the S7-1500 platform was required to future-proof the plant's automation infrastructure while maintaining production continuity with minimal disruption.
