Our Story

Who We Are

A Filipino automation company built on engineering precision.

Watchdog Automation OPC is a Philippine-based industrial automation company specializing in PLC programming, SCADA integration, instrumentation, and control system engineering. We serve clients across power generation, food and beverage, water utilities, and offshore industries.

Our Mission

To deliver automation that works — reliably, precisely, and for the long term.

We are committed to engineering excellence in every project we undertake. Our work is not done when the system is installed — it is done when the client is confident operating it independently.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted automation partner in the Philippines.

Known for technical expertise, integrity, and long-term client relationships — we want every client to feel that choosing Watchdog was one of the best decisions they made for their facility.

Technical Excellence

We engineer solutions that work the first time and last for years.

Integrity

Transparent with clients, realistic with scope, honest about risk.

Partnership

We treat every project as a long-term relationship, not a transaction.

Growth

We invest in our people and stay current with the latest automation technologies.
